    OUR FOCUS

    STAYING TRUE TO THE ORGINALS

    We stay faithful to the original design and storytelling because, like many, we grew up with Half-Life. The stories of Adrian Shephard and Barney Calhoun left a lasting impact on us — and on the game industry as a whole — not just as players, but as fans captivated by the atmosphere, pacing, and world-building that defined these games. Recreating their journeys is our way of honoring what made them so memorable.

    Our focus isn’t to rewrite history, but to retell it — with care, respect, and a few fresh ideas of our own. We’re preserving the core gameplay, puzzles, and tone while enhancing the experience through redesigned levels, improved narrative flow, and expanded gameplay moments that stay faithful to the spirit of the originals.
